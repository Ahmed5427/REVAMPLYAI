What We Examine

A Structured Review of Your Operations

01

Customer Communication

How leads and customers are managed from first contact through to delivery and follow-up. Where response times slow and where automation can accelerate.

02

Lead Management

How leads enter your business, how they are tracked, nurtured, and converted. Identifying friction in the sales and follow-up process.

03

Internal Operations

Day-to-day workflows across departments. Where manual tasks consume team time that could be automated or streamlined.

04

Scheduling & Task Management

How appointments, tasks, and coordination happen internally. Where handoffs break down and bottlenecks form.

05

Software Infrastructure

The tools currently in use — how well they integrate, where data gets stuck, and what modern alternatives could unify operations.

06

Reporting & Visibility

How leadership understands business performance. Where visibility is missing and decisions are made without clear data.
